EventsNow

A fullstack web application I created with many features. Some features include event management with ticketing capabilities. This application allows users to attend and host local events. These events can range from categories in Music, Food & Drinks, Classes, Arts, Parties, Sport & Wellness, and Networking. Users can also sell, buy and keep track of their purchased tickets from these events. This project was inspired by Eventbrite.

Tetris

Everybody has played tetris. It may not be the video game, but you had to definitely play it while packing your car or your luggage! I was able to recreate this classic game using just JavaScript and Canvas. Implementation of fast drop, next piece, and rotational were absolutely essential. The pieces are pure random and the difficulty increases the better you get.
